## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address, Second Life username, display name
- **Profile Information:** Avatar image, bio, interest groups, grid status
- **Content:** Posts, comments, images, captions, tags
- **Social Interactions:** Follows, likes, saved posts
- **Wallet Information:** InCynq credit balance (L$) and transaction history
- **Communications:** Messages and support requests

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Location Data:** Approximate location for local features (when permitted)
- **Cookies:** Session cookies for authentication (see Cookie Policy)

### 2.3 Future Analytics & Referral Tracking

**Brand Analytics (Future):**
We plan to introduce analytics for brand accounts to measure engagement, including:
- Post views and reach
- Interaction rates (likes, comments, shares)
- Follower demographics (aggregated and anonymized)
- Engagement trends

**Note:** These analytics will only be available to brand account holders for their own content and will not share individual user identities.

**Referral Program:**
We track referral relationships when you invite friends to join InCynq:
- Your unique referral code
- Which users signed up using your referral link
- Activation status and reward payment history
- Referral earnings (L$ credited to your wallet)

This data is used solely to manage the referral program, credit rewards, and enforce program limits. We do not share your referral activity with third parties.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- **Provide Services:** Create and manage your account, display your profile and content
- **Social Features:** Enable following, liking, commenting, and discovery
- **Communication:** Send account notifications, updates, and support responses
- **Personalization:** Customize your feed based on interests and connections
- **Safety & Security:** Prevent fraud, abuse, and protect user safety
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
- **Future Analytics:** Provide engagement metrics to brand accounts (anonymized)
- **Referral Program:** Track referrals, credit rewards, and prevent abuse

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

- **Contract Performance:** To provide our services as agreed
- **Legitimate Interests:** Platform security, fraud prevention, service improvement
- **Consent:** For optional features like location sharing and analytics
- **Legal Obligation:** To comply with applicable laws

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Public Information

Your profile information (username, display name, avatar, bio) and posts are **publicly visible** to other InCynq users unless you set your account to "Hidden" (discoverable = false).

### 5.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with:
- **Supabase:** Database and authentication hosting
- **Cloudflare:** Content delivery and security
- **Second Life API:** To verify usernames and fetch avatars (optional)

All providers are GDPR-compliant and process data on our behalf.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.

### 5.4 We Do NOT

- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers (for now)
- Track you across other websites

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

As an EU resident, you have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your data
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a structured format
- **Object:** Opt out of certain data processing
- **Withdraw Consent:** For consent-based processing

**To exercise your rights:** Email legal@incynq.app

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data:

- **Active Accounts:** While your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Up to 30 days after deletion for backup/recovery
- **Legal Requirements:** Longer if required by law

## 8. Children's Privacy

InCynq is intended for Second Life residents who are 18+ (or 16+ with parental consent in their region). We do not knowingly collect data from children under the minimum age.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Supabase servers (EU region where possible). Transfers outside the EU use standard contractual clauses and GDPR safeguards.

## 10. Security

We use industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption (HTTPS, database encryption)
- Secure authentication (hashed passwords)
- Access controls and monitoring
- Regular security audits

However, no system is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and report suspicious activity.
